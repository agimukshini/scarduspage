Community Contributions

FLOSSK Partnership

We proudly partner with FLOSSK, a nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting Free/Libre and Open Source Software in Kosovo and the broader region. Through this partnership, we contribute to the development of the local tech community and education initiatives.

Our contributions include providing a free network laboratory for students to gain hands-on experience with real-world infrastructure. This lab serves as a training ground where students can experiment with networking equipment, security tools, and infrastructure management in a safe, supportive environment.

Additionally, we periodically host training sessions covering development, infrastructure, and cybersecurity topics. These sessions are designed to share our expertise with the next generation of IT professionals and give back to the community that has supported our growth.
