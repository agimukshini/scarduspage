About CMMC

The Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) is a unified standard for implementing cybersecurity across the Defense Industrial Base (DIB). It combines various cybersecurity standards into a single certification framework to ensure that contractors implement appropriate safeguards to protect sensitive information.

CMMC addresses both Federal Contract Information (FCI) and Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) as stipulated in FAR or DFARS clauses. Organizations must implement appropriate cybersecurity measures based on the requirements specified in their contracts.

Understanding NIST provisions is crucial for CMMC compliance. The framework incorporates cybersecurity standards from NIST Special Publication 800-171 and other sources, ensuring that defense contractors implement appropriate safeguards to protect both FCI and CUI as required by FAR and DFARS clauses.
